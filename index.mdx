title: "Introduction"
description: "Skygen is an AI agent automation platform that runs on real devices, Cloud PCs, and sandboxed environments."
---

Skygen lets you build and run AI agents that control computers. Connect your laptop or spin up a Cloud PC, chat with an agent, and it will click, type, browse, and use apps on your behalf. Integrations with 500+ third-party services (via MCP) let agents read your email, update your CRM, or post to Slack while they work.
